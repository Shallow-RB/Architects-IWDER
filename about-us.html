ABOUT US

Architects are a British metalcore band from Brighton. Formed in 2004. The band currently consists of vocalist Sam Carter, drummer Dan Searle, bassist Alex Dean and guitarists Adam Christianson and Josh Middleton. They have released seven studio albums, under which our latest album For Those That Wish To Exist.

Architects have been summarised as being "pumped with both controlled rage and unhindered heart, accessible and ambitious, aggressive and beautiful".

Considered as offering metalcore style to more mainstream audiences they have been called by various critics as metalcore, mathcore, post-hardcore, progressive metal, post-metalcore and technical metal. Their music is considered as having choppy, complex guitar riffs, the use of obscure time signatures and rhythmic breakdowns, and for their guitarists swapping between a "down-tuned rumble" and "melodic punk" during songs. However the band's music isn't solely been based upon technicality and does use catchy riffs and choruses."

Concerts reviewed as "Breathtaking, a wild ride and once in a life time experience." We try to deliver our music in the best possible way so that our audience can fully understand the sweat and tears we put into each and every track.
